GROWTH

How does this work represent an area of growth for you? Cite specific examples.

This work represents my own personal growth because it shows that I learned a lot over a short course of time. My use of complex code, such as conditional statements, is something that I wouldn’t even have considered at the beginning of the school year.

What were the hardest areas for you to master and why?

The hardest thing that I have had to master is coding a website based on only written code rather than block code. This was difficult to learn because I had grown so accustomed to using block code. Still, I think that I have picked up this new skill very well.

What obstacles did you face/ How did you get past those obstacles?

The main obstacle that I faced time and time again this school year was that there would be small problems in my code that I would look over, and they would hurt the outcome of whatever project I was working on. I moved past this obstacle by adopting the “rubber ducky” method. This involves explaining your code to someone else.

How did your level of effort in class or on this specific project directly influence your success?

I feel that I put a lot of effort into all the projects that I did this year, and that is visible in the outcome of them.

Knowing what you know now, is there anything you would change or improve?

I do not think I would change the way that I learned anything this year.
